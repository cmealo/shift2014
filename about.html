Beyond Art + Science

What is creativity really? How about genius?

Shift is a three-day conference that moves beyond traditional ideas that divide the arts and the sciences to explore the makings of truly inspired ideas and discoveries. From creative genius, to eccentricity and madness, Shift takes attendees on a captivating journey through the processes and connections that drive insight across all domains. Interdisciplinary and collaborative to the core, Shift gathers a vibrant spectrum of speakers from a wide range of fields to lead the evolution from right vs. left into a more unified philosophy of creative problem solving.
