6.18.14

Creative Genius

The first day of the conference focuses on the idea of the creative genius. First by defining and tracing the origins of creativity, and then by examining the concept of genius, day one breaks down underlying concepts and vocabulary that impart creative insight. The final portion of the day previews the merge between the arts and sciences and some of the unique fields and projects born of this union.

6.19.14

Disinhibition

The second day of the conference is inspired by a core concept of creativity—disinihibition—an unhindered, and occasionally dangerous, ability to recognize connections in disparate things. A day of madness and magnificence ensues with the inspection of the dark side of creativity, transitioning into an investigation of the ‘functional schizotype’ and healthy ways of becoming creatively disinhibited and maintaining balance.

6.2O.14

Applied Education

The final day of the conference focuses on education—both of oneself and of others. This day begins with self-education, concentrating on ways to ignite inner creativity and overcome real and perceived obstacles that hold many back. A systems approach to creative, and adaptive, problem solving methodologies is explored before examining ways to foster creative thinking in others. The day culminates with presentations about the value of collaborative learning in addition to a review of collaborative resources and projects both existent and in-the-works.
